Welcome to mrahmedcomputing.co.uk. The resources on this website can be used to support the teaching and learning of the KS3, KS4 and KS5 national Computer Science curriculum in the UK. Resources are updated frequently to match current specifications, and have been used since 2015 to support strong outcomes amongst Computer Science students. Both AQA and OCR Computer Science specifications are supported on this website.
